Quacky Bot Staff Policy

As a Quacky Bot Staff Member, not only are you subject to the Same Rules as other Members, the rules are more heavily enforced.

Staff Permission Abuse

If you abuse your permissions as Quacky Bot Staff, you will get a Staff Warning.

Getting 2 Staff Warnings will result in a demotion or removal from the staff team.

If you get another Staff Warning you will be banned from the Staff Team.

Breaking Rules as Staff

If you get 2 normal warnings while being a Quacky Bot Staff Member, you will be demoted or removed from the staff team.

Getting another 2 warnings will result in you getting banned from the staff team.
