Vote for Quacky Bot

Voting for Quacky helps Show your Support for Quacky Bot! When you vote, you will get additional bot perks for showing your support.

Voting on top.gg will get you the following Vote Perks:

  • The "Voted" role for 1.5 Days as long as you're in the Quacky Support Server.
  • The "Voted" Badge on your Profile for the next 12 hours.
  • Access to the -stats and -uptime command.
