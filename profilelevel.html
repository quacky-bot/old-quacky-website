Quacky Profile Level

What is the Quacky Profile Level

The Quacky Profile Level is based on different Checks to see if you are a Quacky Community Member.

What are these Checks?

The checks used to decide your "Quacky Profile Level" are:

  1. Found at least 1 Error with Quacky. (Bug Badge)
  2. Voted for Quacky or is a Staff Member, Donator, or Special Rank.
  3. In the Quacky Support Server.
  4. Made a Suggestion that has been Approved. (Suggester Badge)

What are the Different Levels I can Get?

Based on the amount of checks that have passed you can get one of the following levels:

  • 0 Checks - Common
  • 1 Check - Silver
  • 2 Checks - Gold
  • 3 Checks - Diamond
  • 4 Checks - Platinum
