User Feedback Privacy Policy

By submitting Feedback to the Quacky Team via Google Forms, you agree to the following privacy policy.

What information is stored?

We store your e-mail on the form.

We store your Discord userid, name and discriminator so that we can better collect data!

Why we store the information and how we use it.

This information is stored in order for us to contact you, should you allow via the answer on the form. This information is also stored to provide us statistics to better improve Quacky.

Who gets this data?

The Administrators of the Quacky Discord Bot have full access to view data submitted via the Google Forms.

3rd Party Data Sharing

We use Google Forms by Google LLC to handle and manage all data submitted to us for Feedback.

Google LLC collects personally identifiable information such as Google Account information. You may view their privacy policy here.

Questions and Concerns.

If you are concerned about the data stored Join the Quacky Support Server or DM DuckMasterAl#0001 on Discord.

How to Remove your data.

If you would like to remove your data, you can Join the Quacky Support Server and type "!new" in a commands channel to open a Ticket!

If you are banned from the support server, DM DuckMasterAl#0001 on Discord to remove the data.


Note: We reserve the right to change this without notifying our users.
