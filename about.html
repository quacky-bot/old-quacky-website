About Quacky Bot

What is Quacky Bot?

Quacky is a moderation bot with lots of utility features. Quacky has custom moderation features like Voice and Text Channel Moderation to make life easier for your Moderators.

What makes Quacky different than other Moderation Bots?

Quacky has the following unique features:

  1. Text Channel Moderation
    • Channel banning to remove people from text channels.
    • Channel adding to add people to text channels.
  2. Voice Channel Moderation
    • Locking/Unlocking Voice Channels.
    • Voice Channel Kicking to kick everyone out of a voice channel.
    • Voice Channel Moving to move everyone from one voice channel to a different one.
    • Voice Channel Muting to mute everyone in a voice channel without Server Muting them.
    • Voice Channel Spectating to make it so that new people that join the channel can't speak.
  3. Silent Moderation Messages
    • Silently kick, ban, and mute someone so you aren't shown as the Moderator.
